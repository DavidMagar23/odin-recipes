Protein shake

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 scoop chocolate-flavored protein powder
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder, or more to taste

Steps

  1. First, fill a blender with milk.
  2. Then, put some banans in it.
  3. According to your weight and need, insert your protein powder.
  4. After that,you can adjust and put peanut butter, honey, and cocoa powder,etc in the blender.
  5. Lastly, blend until smooth!
